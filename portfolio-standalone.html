Backend-first, full-stack by habit.

I'm a B.Tech Computer Science student at Buddha Institute of Technology, Gorakhpur, graduating in 2027. Currently Java Full Stack Developer who enjoys the part of building software most people skip past — the data model, the API contract, the error case nobody planned for. Spring Boot and Hibernate are where I spend most of my time, turning requirements into services that are predictable under load and easy for the next developer to read.
